About Us

Guru Harikrishn Foundation is a platform that supports Cancer Treatment in India with its philosophy laid down in Treatment & Research

Background

Cancer is rising rapidly every year with around 2.5 million patients & every year 700 thousand people being affected reportedly and the deaths caused due to cancer has risen to more than 5.5 lac

Lack of awareness on part of patients who fail to understand their symptoms and failure of timely and appropriate intervention, lead to the cancer presenting at advanced stages leading to large number of deaths

Majority of cancers are curable if detected and treated timely. Government, NGOs and other agencies have made laudable efforts in spreading awareness on its detection and prevention. But what after early detection?

Challenges

Public sector hospitals are still the only option for majority of cancer patients but there exists a huge gap between the increasing number of cancer patients and the treatment facilities available across the country

Private sector hospitals provide almost all forms of advanced cancer treatment, but is comparatively expensive than the Public Sector Hospitals which makes it out of bounds for majority of our population

Health Insurance is still limited and a very less percentage of population is actually covered for oncology treatment


The answer to the above challenges is the Umeed Initiative of Guru Harikrishn Foundation

Programs

UMEED is an initiative to bridge this huge gap between patients & treatment facilities by providing timely and quality treatment to the patients irrespective of their financial capacity to afford the same. In the present form, UMEED provides treatment for the most common Cancers in India. Around 98 patients have taken the benefit of Umeed Initiative till date


60 end stage cancer patients treated free of cost under the Umeed initiative
