About Us

Guru Harikrishn Foundation is a platform that provides support to facilitate cancer treatment & research to improve survival. Till date, we have reached to a lot of cancer patients and treated them. With your support, we plan to reach out to many more patients to provide timely treatment. To read the full story behind Guru Harikrishn Foundation, click here.


Background

Cancer is rising rapidly every year with around 2.5 million patients & every year 700 thousand people being affected reportedly, and the deaths caused due to cancer has risen to more than 5.5 lac

Lack of awareness on part of patients who fail to understand the symptoms and failure of timely and appropriate intervention, leads to the cancer presenting at advanced stages leading to large number of deaths

Majority of cancers are curable if detected and treated timely. Government, NGOs and other agencies have made laudable efforts in spreading awareness on its detection. But what after detection?


Challenges

Public sector hospitals being the only option for majority patients, creating a huge gap between the increasing number of patients & the treatment facilities available

The high cost of the private sector hospitals makes them out of reach for majority population, depriving them of advanced cancer treatment

Health Insurance is still limited and a very less percentage of population is actually covered for oncology treatment


The answer to the above challenges is the UMEED Initiative of Guru Harikrishn Foundation

Programs


"UMEED" is an initiative to bridge this huge gap between patients & treatment facilities by providing timely and quality treatment to the patients irrespective of their financial capacity to afford the same. In the present form, UMEED provides treatment for the most common Cancers in India. Around 98 patients have taken the benefit of UMEED Initiative till date


60 end stage cancer patients treated free of cost under the UMEED initiative
